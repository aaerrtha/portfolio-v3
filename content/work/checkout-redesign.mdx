Mobile checkout was losing users at nearly every step. Cart review felt cluttered, shipping options were hard to compare, and error states often appeared too late to recover the session.

<CaseImage alt="Checkout flow overview placeholder" caption="Replace with a key screen from the redesigned checkout experience." />

## The Problem

Analytics showed most abandonment happened after users entered shipping details. Interviews revealed that unexpected fees, unclear delivery timelines, and repetitive form fields made the process feel longer than it was.

## Approach

The redesign focused on confidence and momentum:

- Collapsed the flow into fewer visible steps with a persistent order summary
- Surfaced delivery dates and fees earlier in the journey
- Introduced inline validation and clearer recovery states for failed payments
- Tested copy and hierarchy with quick moderated sessions after each release
